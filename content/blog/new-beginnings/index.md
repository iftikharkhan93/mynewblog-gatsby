A lot of factors such as light, subjects, colors, etc., go into creating a compelling and effective photograph and one of the most important factors that need to be given serious consideration is composition. A photograph can go from good to great by making use of the right compositional guidelines that suit the scenario and the story in the photo.

## Composition in photography

Composition in photography refers to how the objects or elements in a photograph are arranged or placed so it creates a sense of balance and helps keep the viewer engaged. Depending on the compositional guideline used, the composition should take the viewer straight to the main point of interest or guide the viewer along with the different elements in the image to the point of interest.

- Photographers do not need to stick to only one compositional guideline in a photograph but can combine more than one to create a powerful image. 
- Here are some images that make use of brilliant compositional guidelines. If you are a beginner, this list will help you to observe each image and learn the techniques used to make each image powerful and visually appealing.
